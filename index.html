About the Token

Paradizlight (PLZ) is an innovative token built on the Polygon network. It features an automatic burn mechanism that reduces the circulating supply over time, increasing rarity and long-term value.

Tokenomics

Initial Supply: 100,000,000 PLZ
Decimals: 5
Burn Mechanism: Burns 36.9% every 6 months until only 1,000,000 PLZ remain in circulation after 5 years.

Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Token launch on Polygon.
  • Phase 2: Development of a unique clock-themed application.
  • Phase 3: Integration within the ecosystem with DAO, rewards, and staking features.
  • Phase 4: Project expansion and community growth.
