PARADIZlight (PLZ) Whitepaper

Native utility token for real-world needs — "We think about you."

1. Introduction

PARADIZlight (PLZ) is a deflationary utility token built on the Polygon (PoS) blockchain, designed to power a cutting-edge wallet application integrating NFC payments, EVM-compatible token transfers, and a vibrant ecosystem of rewards and social impact. PLZ aims to combine seamless usability with community governance and real-world value.

2. Vision & Mission

Our mission is to create a token that is deeply integrated into daily digital transactions, fostering user engagement through cashback rewards, gamification, and charitable contributions. We prioritize transparency, security, and sustainability to build trust and drive adoption.

3. Tokenomics

Total Supply and Decimals

The total supply of PLZ is fixed at 100,000,000 tokens, with 5 decimals for fine granularity.

Token Distribution

  • 35% — Frozen (DAO Reserve): Locked for long-term governance, ecosystem stability, and sustainability, with gradual unlocking per vesting schedule.
  • 20% — Operations & Cashback Fund: Used for rewarding users, cashback bonuses, airdrops, and supporting in-app transactions to stimulate active usage.
  • 15% — Development Fund: Dedicated to ongoing development, partnerships, integrations, and platform growth.
  • 10% — Rewards & Gamification: Allocated for NFT rewards, achievements, and gamified user engagement.
  • 10% — Charity & Aid: Set aside for social good initiatives and charitable contributions via the platform.
  • 10% — Liquidity Pool: Initial liquidity on decentralized exchanges like SushiSwap to ensure smooth trading.

Deflationary Mechanisms

  • 1% Burn on Every Transaction: Automatically reduces circulating supply, increasing scarcity over time.
  • Scheduled Burns: Periodic burning of liquidity pool tokens and tokens from burn wallets to maintain deflationary pressure.

4. Utility & Ecosystem

  • NFC Payments: PLZ is the default token for NFC payments within the PARADIZlight wallet.
  • Cashback Rewards: Users earn up to 3% cashback in PLZ for using the wallet and transacting with the token.
  • Gamification: Earn NFTs, badges, and other rewards for active participation and engagement.
  • DAO Governance: PLZ holders can vote on platform proposals and participate in community governance — 1 PLZ equals 1 vote.
  • Charitable Giving: The platform integrates charity features, allowing users to contribute to social causes with PLZ tokens.

5. Price & Growth Strategy

The initial price per PLZ token is set at $0.05. We anticipate value growth driven by:

  • Ongoing deflationary burning mechanisms.
  • Growing demand from in-app usage, rewards, and cashback incentives.
  • Locked DAO reserves ensuring supply stability and governance participation.
  • Increasing community adoption and ecosystem expansion.

6. Transparency & Security

All transactions and token data are recorded on-chain via Polygon, accessible and auditable by all users. The smart contract address and burn statistics are openly shared, fostering trust and confidence.

7. Roadmap

  • Q4 2025: Charity platform integration and additional partnerships.
  • 2026 and beyond: Continued ecosystem growth, multi-chain support, and expansion of token utility.
